Privacy Policy

Qbee520 Website & Android Applications

Important: This privacy policy applies to the Qbee520 website (qbee520.github.io) and all Android applications developed and published by Qbee520.

Last Updated: February 2026

Qbee520 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit our website or use our Android applications ("Apps" or "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage statistics to improve app functionality and performance
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for crash reports and app optimization
  • App Data: Each app stores settings and user preferences locally as needed for functionality

1.2 Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit personal data to external servers
  • Data does not leave your device

1.3 Health Data (Blood Sugar Tracker & Pedometer)

Some of our apps access and collect health-related data:

  • Blood Sugar Tracker: Collects blood glucose readings that you manually enter, along with measurement timestamps and optional notes. This data is used solely to display your personal health trends and history within the app.
  • Pedometer: Collects step count data measured by your device's built-in sensors. This data is used solely to display your daily activity progress within the app.
  • All health data is stored exclusively on your device. It is never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.
  • You have full control over your health data at all times.

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain app services and functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Monitor app performance and stability

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We adhere to the following principles:

  • No Sale to Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties
  • Limited Sharing: Information is only shared when legally required or necessary to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: We may work with trusted service providers only under strict confidentiality agreements

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Play Services

Our apps may use Google Play Services, which includes:

  • App updates and security
  • Crash reporting and analytics
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Advertising Services

Our website uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies (including the DART cookie) and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

If you have not opted out of third-party ad serving, cookies of other third-party vendors or ad networks may also be used to serve ads on our site.

4.3 Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: To remember your preferences and settings

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Data Security

We implement:

  • Industry-standard security measures
  • Restricted data access
  • Regular monitoring for security vulnerabilities
  • Minimal data collection principles

6. Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All apps are family-friendly
  • No inappropriate content for children
  • Parents with concerns should contact qbee520@gmail.com

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what data has been collected about you
  • Delete: Remove local data by uninstalling the app
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out: Opt out of data collection activities

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention Periods:

  • App settings and preferences are retained on your device until the app is uninstalled or data is manually cleared
  • Health Data (Blood Sugar Tracker): Blood glucose records are retained on your device until you manually delete them within the app or uninstall the app
  • Health Data (Pedometer): Step count history is retained on your device until you manually delete it within the app or uninstall the app
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for up to 12 months for analysis purposes

How to Delete Your Data:

  • Within the App: Each app provides data management options in its settings. You can delete individual records or clear all data directly from within the app.
  • Via Android Settings: Navigate to Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Storage > Clear Data to remove all app data from your device.
  • By Uninstalling: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Contact Us: You may also contact us at qbee520@gmail.com to request data deletion assistance.

9. International Data Transfers

Since data is primarily stored locally on your device, international transfers are minimized. When transfers occur, appropriate safeguards are in place.

  • New feature additions
  • Changes in legal requirements
  • Improvements in industry best practices
  • User feedback

Significant changes will be communicated to users through app updates.

Your privacy is important to us, and we take all inquiries seriously.
